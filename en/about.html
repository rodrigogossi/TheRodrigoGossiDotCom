About me

Hello, my name is Rodrigo Gossi. I work in the technology ecosystem, building digital products, stable APIs, and fast interfaces.

Why does this site exist?

The modern web has become extremely heavy, cluttered with cookie consent pop-ups, advertising trackers, and JS frameworks with tens of megabytes just to display simple paragraphs of text.

This site follows a different philosophy:

  • Independence: The files belong to me. They do not depend on any centralized blogging platform. I can zip them up and publish them to any server in minutes.
  • Lightweight: Ultra-fast load times, built entirely with basic HTML and CSS, with subtle touches of JavaScript.
  • Readability: Focus on proper contrast, comfortable typography, and a total absence of distractions.
