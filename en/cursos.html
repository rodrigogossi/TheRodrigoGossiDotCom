Criação audiovisual na prática: crie seu primeiro treinamento

During this training we will explore various aspects of the audiovisual world, in order to learn all the steps necessary to produce our own training course!

We will cover topics such as:

  • Creative writing;
  • Scriptwriting and Structuring;
  • Audio editing;
  • Color correction;
  • Video editing principles;

Ready to start your journey in the incredible world of audiovisuals?

Introdução a IA's generativas

During this course we will learn to use the most popular AI tools existing today. We will learn to use them to create and format text, generate images, and make illustrations that we can use, for example, to illustrate stories or presentations. We will also learn how to transform text into voice, to use as narration for a podcast or video.

All of this without needing any prior knowledge, starting from scratch until we get our hands dirty and use artificial intelligences to create our materials.
